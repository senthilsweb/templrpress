# Hello, world

This is a placeholder blog post that ships with TemplrPress.

The blog is **disabled by default**. Enable it by setting `blog.enabled: true`
in your `config.yaml` and flipping the matching navigation entry.

Drop more `.md` files into `content/blog/` and they'll appear here, sorted by
date (newest first).
